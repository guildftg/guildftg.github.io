Loot System

Our EP/GP system is consistent, rewards effort, attendance, and meaningful contribution.

  • Effort Points (EP): earned for raid participation and guild contributions
  • Gear Points (GP): track loot received
  • Priority Ratio (PR): EP ÷ GP determines loot priority

Fair & Transparent

Loot is distributed based on contribution and clear rules.

  • Priority reflects effort and need, not favoritism
  • Rules are consistent across all raid teams
  • Tiered auctions and soft reserve give players control while maintaining fairness

Raid Structure

A 25-man progression core is supported by multiple 10-man teams, allowing for both performance-driven progression and development opportunities for new or returning players.

Leadership

Our officer team has clear responsibilities, maintains accountability, and ensures raids and guild systems operate smoothly. Leadership is structured to support both players and long-term guild stability.

Player Development

We invest in players who want to improve, take on responsibility, and push content seriously. Guidance, mentoring, and constructive feedback are available for those willing to put in the work.

Expectations

Come prepared, communicate clearly, and respect the team. Reliability, preparation, and a positive approach to raids ensure everyone can perform and enjoy the game together.

About FTG

FTG is a raiding guild built on stability, consistency, and shared responsibility. We combine structured leadership, intentional raid systems, and a supportive community culture to create an environment where players can perform, improve, and enjoy the game without unnecessary friction. Our focus is on reliable execution, clear expectations, and maintaining a guild where effort, preparation, and respect are recognized and rewarded.
