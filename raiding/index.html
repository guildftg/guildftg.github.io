Welcome

Welcome to the FTG raid team! These expectations are designed to keep raids efficient, fair, and enjoyable for everyone.

Our goal is simple: kill bosses, distribute loot fairly, and have a good time doing it.

📝 Sign-Up & Roster

  • Sign-ups are posted in Discord for all 40-man raids
  • You must sign up to be considered
  • Roster is posted at least 24 hours before raid
  • Roster based on:
    • Raid composition needs
    • Previous priority (carryover / standby)
    • Character performance
  • Final decisions are at the raid leader’s discretion

🏆 Raid Priority

  • Warlords and Generals have priority spots
  • General rank = long-term dedication and contribution
  • Earned through:
    • Helping others
    • Consistent participation
    • Building guild relationships

🎒 Come Prepared

  • Consumables & buffs strongly recommended
  • Gear must be fully repaired
  • Know boss mechanics before raid
  • Ask questions if unsure — preparation matters

⏱️ Attendance & Timing

  • Be in raid 15 minutes early
  • Late players without notice will be replaced
  • Notify status in Discord if delayed

🎧 During the Raid

  • Discord required (listening is mandatory)
  • Be a team player — cooperate and communicate
  • Maintain a positive attitude
  • Respect raid leader decisions
  • Constructive input is welcome — respectfully

🪑 Standby (Bench)

  • Non-rostered players may be placed on standby
  • Must be available 15 minutes before raid
  • Remain available for entire raid duration
  • Can be online, on alt, or reachable via Discord

📊 Standby EP Rules

  • Receive 50% EP if available
  • No EP if:
    • You miss availability checks
    • You cannot join when called
  • Availability checks occur periodically

📈 After the Raid

  • Logs will be posted after each raid
  • Reviewing performance is encouraged
  • Coaching available from officers and experienced players

📌 Two-Raid Etiquette (Updated 6/25/2025)

  • Main characters get priority for raid spots
  • Alts should sign up as BENCH
  • This ensures fair roster distribution

  • Bench Points Rule:
  • No double dipping:
    • If benched Tuesday and raid Thursday → no Tuesday bench EP
  • Bench lists tracked manually across raid cycle
